A Marathi Community in Wales



About Us

We are a social, non-political, community-driven organisation for all Marathi who are also residents of Wales. We are run by the members of our community coming together to celebrate our cultural traditions.

Member Perks

All members are able to buy tickets to events at a concessional price. Members children (under age 12) can avail Marathi language lessons for free

Marathi Shala

We run Marathi language lessons for all age groups every 2 weeks. Email us to find out more. Non-member children can join for suggestion donation amount.
